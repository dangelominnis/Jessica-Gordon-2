My Book

Healing Through Your Hurt is a 40 day inspirational devotional of prayers, personal testimonies, and scriptures inspired by Jessica Gordon’s healing process through marriage, divorce, and relationships with family and friends.

Devotional

The devotional is to encourage anyone who thinks they're alone in any type of painful situation, and how to overcome their hurt while remaining a servant of God.

Purpose

Healing Through Your Hurt reminds people that God has a purpose for everyone and to trust God in any situation. As you trust God, you truly start to see him.

Meet Jessica

Jessica R Gordon - Author & Christian

Jessica Gordon was born in Texarkana, TX. An anointed woman of God, she knew she had a special calling on her life ever since she was sixteen. She is a free-spirited, passionate, and warm-hearted Christian. Jessica loves encouraging people in need and doing ministry in her church and community. In 2017, Jessica accepted her calling from Jesus Christ.

She has a Bachelor’s degree in Religious Studies from Arkansas Baptist College, and she is a member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc. Jessica currently lives in Little Rock, AR, where she is an active member at St. Mark Baptist Church. She completed her certification in Master Life.

Jessica is also a part of the Associate Ministers group, Roddy E. Collins School of Ministry, and Women's II Sunday School Class at St. Mark Baptist Church. Jessica enjoys reading spiritual books,studying the bible, and spending time with her two daughters.
